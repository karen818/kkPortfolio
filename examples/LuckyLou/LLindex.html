Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you ship Lucky Lou Shoes?

We ship our shoes anywhere in the world! When you go through the checkout, you will be asked for your shipping address and our website will automatically calculate the correct shipping charge for your location. We ship via USPS (United States Postal Service) Priority for all US orders and USPS First Class for orders to Canada and overseas.

Shipping via USPS First Class is a reliable option that includes a tracking number, but DOES NOT include insurance. We've chosen this option in order to significantly reduce shipping prices for our overseas and Canadian customers, but we are not responsible for any missed deliveries. That said, it's still a very reliable way to ship. If you prefer to have your shoes shipped via USPS Priority, which does include insurance, please contact us before you go through the checkout and we will send you a Paypal invoice reflecting the upgrade in shipping.



Can I return or exchange my Lucky Lou Shoes?

We offer exchanges or credit on any purchases, as long as the shoes are in perfect, unworn condition and in the original packaging. If an exchange is needed due to the incorrect size being chosen, customer will pay for shipping both ways. If the exchange is due to a manufacturer's defect that somehow escaped our scrutiny, Lucky Lou will pay for shipping both ways.

We now also offer returns. You must contact us within 7 days after receiving your shoes if you would like to return them for any reason. The shoes must reach us in perfect, unworn condition and in the original packaging before we process your refund.

What are your payment options?

We currently accept Paypal and credit cards. When you go through our checkout, you will be directed to the Paypal website. However, you do NOT have to use a Paypal account to pay. You will be given the option to use your Paypal account (if you have one) or a regular debit or credit card. If you would like to pay for your purchase directly, you can call us at 512-619-0636 and we will take a credit card over the phone.



Are there variations because they are handmade?

All of our shoes are hand-carved, so there may be some very slight variations in the final product. Our straps are also hand-cut, so there may be some very slight differences in the exact patterns. We do our best to make sure that any variations are minimal.



What size shoes do you offer?

Our shoes are all handmade, so there may be some very slight variations in sizing. Additionally, there is no real worldwide standard of measurement for footwear. Our shoes do run true to size in length and width. If you're in doubt as to which size to order, please don't hesitate to contact us with any questions.
